Barracks

Painless IoT Fleet Management

Why Barracks?

Barracks provides painless IoT fleet management. Wether you are building your first connected object or have tens of millions of devices, we will help you manage your software updates.

Barracks SDKs

We are happy to offer you free and open-source SDKs for the many platforms you may be using! Have a look at our awesome documentation and start integrating our service in your apps:

  • Android Client: docs
  • iOS / OSX Client: docs
  • Python Client
  • Javascript / NodeJS Client

Bug reports and enhancements

If you happen to find a bug, or feel the need to have new features in our SDKs, feel free to submit an issue or a pull request in the related project.

Support

We will not provide support through Github, though feel free to ask for help on StackOverflow, using the 'barracks' tag.
